Guidance for registering for workshops

All workshops have the option to attend in person or remotely. Please note that you will need to opt for a method of delivery at the point of registation.

Registration

Please register your interest in a particular course by completing this form. This does not guarantee you a place on your chosen course. Places will be allocated on a first come first served basis once registration closes and you will be asked to re-confirm your availability to attend. Registration dates are listed for each workshop below. If the form is not accepting responses, this means that there are no workshops open for registration at this time.

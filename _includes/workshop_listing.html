Guidance for registering for workshops

All workshops have the option to attend in person or remotely. Please note that you will need to opt for a method of delivery at the point of registation.

The process for signing up for a workshop differs depending on whether you are a PhD student or a member of staff.

PhD students: booking is handled by the Doctoral College via Inkpath. You wil need to register for an account. Once you are logged in the links below should take you direct to the relevant page.

Staff: booking is handled by Researcher Development via iTrent. The links below should take you direct to the relevant page.
